About AI Triage
How It Works

Our AI-powered triage system uses natural language processing and machine learning to analyze patient-reported symptoms and provide an initial assessment of severity and recommended action.

Severity Levels
  • Low Minor conditions that typically require routine care
  • Medium Conditions requiring prompt attention but not emergent
  • High Serious conditions requiring urgent medical attention
  • Critical Life-threatening conditions requiring immediate emergency care
Important: This tool is meant to assist healthcare providers and should not replace professional medical judgment. All AI assessments must be reviewed by qualified medical staff.
